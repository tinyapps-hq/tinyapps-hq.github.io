# v3 Carnoustie


### 3.3.0
Released Jul-19, 2026

Our biggest update yet\! This version brings new game modes, new trip planning features, and an all-new web experience for hosts to make configuring and running your trips even easier.

* Added the [Host Portal](https://app.twoup.golf) so you can configure your tournament from anywhere  
* You can now share a live leaderboard link with anyone. Pull it up on your phone, your laptop, or a TV at your club\!  
* Added an itinerary module so that you can lay out the schedule of events for everyone, including travel, meals, golf, and more. And, it stays updated showing the next event  
* Added three new game modes:  
  * High-Low: Team match play where you match each team’s lowest score on a hole against each other, and the highest. Each hole is worth 2 points, and modern scoring allows for big swings in momentum. A crowd favorite that keeps everyone in the match the whole round\!  
  * Stroke Play: Our number one requested feature is now available\! Record your stroke play scores and compete in an 18-hole match where the lowest score takes all  
  * High-Low Nassau: High Low broken into 3 separate matches–front, back, and overall  
* Launched [twoup.golf](https://twoup.golf) as a marketing/landing site  
* A player’s record now counts Nassau points; not just overall wins and losses  
* Added “Most Paired With” record to Clubs  
* The tournament setup wizard now prompts you to randomize pairings, making tournament setup faster and easier than ever  
* Assigning players to matches now lists the players who don’t yet have a match in this session first, so that you can easily see who’s left and you don’t have to scroll to find the right players  
* Bug fixes:  
  * Nassau notifications are now more useful  
  * Nassau matches receive streak notifications  
  * Changing a session’s type of play now immediately updates all the matches in that sessions, preventing confusion where the match was empty or unscored  
  * Player records now respect the team colors in the event, instead of falling back to blue and red  
  * Fixed the header button colors with liquid glass on iOS
